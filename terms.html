Redesign Terms

  1. These changes are considered redesigns and MUST be submitted for reapproval:
    • Adding/tweaking/removing TRAITED characteristics. Link to the appropriate shop item. Traited characteristics include the horns, halo, tendency, etc.
    • Body type edits. Changes must be within reason. Drastic changes in body type, such as making plus-sized characters thin, will NOT be approved.
    • Skin tone edits. Skin tone changes are allowed, as long as the new skin tone is within the same range of the old one. Whitewashing or drastically changing a character's race is NOT allowed. This does not apply to a skin tone looking altered due to the nature of a piece, like a scene with dramatic blue lighting.
    • Hair color/Haircut edits. If you wish to drastically change a character's hair color or length/style (such as going from a blonde to a redhead), they must be approved and reuploaded onto the Masterlist first. Streaks of color are okay, but it still has to look like the same Auralym.
  2. However these features can be changed at any time without approval:
    • Updating/Redrawing the reference image without changing any traits. Unless you want us to update the Masterlist image.
    • Giving your Auralym different outfits. Unless you want us to update the Masterlist image.
    • Changing the character's gender.
    • Hairstyle edits. You may change how the hair is styled but do not alter the length, cut or color of the hair. For example, putting their hair into a ponytail.
    • Slight color tweaks- this means going from one shade of blue to a similar one, not changing the halo from red to blue.
    • Simplifying a design element OUTSIDE of traits. Such as simplifying heavily patterned clothing, complicated hairstyles, etc.
